About Me

Story

I am originally from Egypt. I was born August 1st 2002 in Sharkia, Egypt. I stayed in Egypt until August of 2012, then my family made the big decision to move to the United states. This was a tough decision and drastically impacted my future. My family and I were only supposed to stay in the United States for three years. However, we ended up staying for four years. My dad's company decided that he needed to go back and work in Egypt. This would be an issue for me because I had grown accustomed to life in the United States. The school cirriculmn was different, and life in Egypt was not good at the time. The second big decision came. I would stay at a boarding school starting at the age of 14 and would complete highscool there. While this might have been difficult for me and my parents, it made it really easy to get into college in the United States. I am now at Texas A&M studying Computer Science!
